New ground to build on

Inspired by the parts of nature that demonstrate emergent self-organization, the Orchid network—an overlay network that runs on the existing internet—is designed for a mutual exchange of benefit in a peer-to-peer marketplace.

How it works

Contributors

  • Relay and bridge nodes stake OXT to earn fees for bandwidth.
  • The stake keeps contributors honest by serving as a decentralized reputation system.
  • The list of nodes is stored in an Ethereum smart contract that is decentralized, accessible, and pen to participation

VPN Users

  • Users connect and configure their VPN service through the app. Probabilistic nano-payments enable purchasing bandwidth on the fly.
  • Orchid uses multi-layer encryption so the more hops you use, the more secure your connection.
  • Users can switch providers depending upon speed, price, location, and availability in the marketplace.
