Episode 36: Lance Vick

We go down the rabbit hole with Lance Vick, Senior Security Engineer at Polychain Capital and the Founder of #!, a group focused on privacy, security, and digital sovereignty. We take a deep dive into the dark side of hacking, how we can avoid the next SolarWinds attack, and the difference between privacy and anonymity.
