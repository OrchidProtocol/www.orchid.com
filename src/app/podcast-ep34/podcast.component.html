Episode 34: Joe Toscano

Go down the rabbit hole with Joe Toscano, featured in the hit documentary “The Social Dilemma”, and co-founder and CIO of BEACON, an organization that operates at the intersection of human-centered design, social impact, and public policy. We take a deep dive into redesigning the online consumer experience, how to break out of the social media echo chamber, and the data privacy reckoning on the horizon.
