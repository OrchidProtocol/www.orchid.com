Episode 20: Neeraj Khandelwal

We go down the rabbit hole with Neeraj Khandelwal, Co-founder of CoinDCX, the largest crypto exchange in India. He shares his story as an early Bitcoin adopter, how his company is opening gateways to new forms of investing, thoughts on the state of defi, and why the blockchain is key to our economic freedom.
